## Phase X: "Society"
**Summary:** Find villagers, build a community, defend against thieves.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Villagers | Nomadic camps with horses (hard to find) |
| Recruiting | Build tent (deer leather + wood + metal) |
| Economy | Money + trading |

**Villager Jobs:**

| Job | Does What |
|-----|-----------|
| Miner | Mines ore/stone/salt |
| Farmer | Farms crops |
| Woodcutter | Chops wood |
| Cook | Makes food |
| Wanderer | Gathers, trades, recruits new villagers |

**Villager Rules:**
- One job each
- Trade with you and each other
- Keep some goods they produce

**Horses:**
- Buy from wanderer villagers
- Tame: Feed + saddle (deer leather + metal strap)

**Key Mechanic:** Villagers do the work, you share the profits
