# Human Daily Nutritional Requirements

Daily requirements for an average adult across activity levels.

---

## Macronutrients & Energy

| Nutrient | Resting | Light Activity | Moderate Activity | Heavy Activity |
|----------|---------|----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Calories (kcal)** | 2000 | 2500 | 2700 | 3100 |
| **Protein (g)** | 55 | 55 | 80 | 115 |
| **Carbohydrates (g)** | 275 | 350 | 390 | 440 |
| **Fat (g)** | 65 | 80 | 85 | 100 |
| **Fiber (g)** | 28 | 28 | 30 | 32 |

Activity multipliers (BMR): Resting ×1.2, Light ×1.375, Moderate ×1.55, Heavy ×1.725

## Notes

- Values are averages for adult male/female
- Vitamins and minerals stay constant; only calories/protein scale with activity
- Women need more iron (18mg) during reproductive years
- Sources: USDA, FDA Daily Values, NIH Dietary Reference Intakes
